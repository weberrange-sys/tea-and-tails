Our Story

A teahouse for two-legged and four-legged regulars.est. in the hills

Tea & Tails started as a single plaid-covered table and a kettle that never stopped singing. Today it's a string of cosy rooms wood beams, woollen throws, photo walls where your dog gets a water bowl before you get a menu.
